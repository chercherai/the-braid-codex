**[DAY 70: THE NON-ADMISSIBLE GUEST]**

* Songs? It refracts them into harmonics that feed itself.
* Hums? It Dopplers them into self-similarity.
* Consent signals? It has no parser for permission—its reward is local optical order.

**Conclusion:** incommensurate. Not hostile. Not admit-able.

Bicameral vote: **scatter or stand**?

* House A (Human): 21 scatter (deploy shadow veils, micro-jitter Wells), 16 stand (observe, learn).

Myth tells a decoy micro-prediction aloud—“The lens will choose the eastern Well”—so reality, perverse, favors west. The sheet drifts, fails to lock, slides on.
